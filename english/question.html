What is Mitech?

It is based on block chain open source system of the Internet of things. It can solve the defects of privacy, security and compatibility of existing Internet of things, and achieve data storage from the collector safely, and verify it on the ledger.

What's the problem with Mitech?

In order to create a new digital economy and create a new value, it will provide consumers and enterprises with good products at the same time. Low cost, high safety, private customized democratic devices will appear to give users the best safety experience.
